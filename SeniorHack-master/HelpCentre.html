Simple Guidelines to Using SeniorHack

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:



A few simple steps:
1. Go to the Service Request page.
2. You can see that 'New Request' has been selected by default.
3. Choose the type of service you wish to request for, fill in the date, time and any additional notes you may want the servicer to take note of.
4. Click the submit button and you're good to go!


At the Service Request page, select 'View Request' and a list of all pending, accepted, cancelled and completed request will be displayed.


Few simple steps:
1. Go to the Service Provider page
2. Scroll down and you'll see a list of service providers with their ratings
3. You can sort by the type of service by clicking on the types of services button.
4. Clicking on the Service provider's profile will direct you to the new Request page if you wish to create new requests.


Only one type of service per request.


Your request will automatically be cancelled if it's not accepted within 7 days, you will then have to make another request for service after cancellation.
